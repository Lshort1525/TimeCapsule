video games

Baldurs Gate is an adventure game based on Dungeons and Dragons. Throughout the game you are given a plethora of options which greatly alter the game. Meaning you can play the game a hundred times and each time has different outcomes.

Hogwarts Legacy is a game based upon the Harry Potter universe. Your charecter harnesses ancient magic which is used to defeat a group of people who are terrorizing the towns and trying to take over.

Lethal Company is a game that you play with other people. The goal is to collect items from alien planets. However, while doing so there are several obstacles and aliens that are trying to kill you.

makeup

Ulta is a store extremely similar to Sephora. Lindsey often shop there to compare prices and get the better deal for my hair, makeup, and skin products. They have exceddingly good nail products especially their press on nails.

Amazon is Lindsey's last resort for makeup products. I usually go there to get cheap products like makeup brushes and other makeup tools that are normally extremely expensive in stores. They often have many options for products which makes it a convinent one stop shop.
