About Lindsey

Lindsey Short is a Graphic Designer who is studying at San Francisco State University. She grew up in Santa Cruz County. From a young age Lindsey has been intrested in the creative arts. For a few years she studied at Sonoma State University to reciever her degree in a communications, however, after studing in Florence Italy for a year she fell deeply in love with the arts which resparked her desire to persue a more art centered career. After some time she decided to finish her degree resulting in her going to San Francisco State.
